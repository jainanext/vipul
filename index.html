Why me?
I led Convention 2023 as a Secretary and Board Member and hosted many Townhalls, Board and Fund-Raising
Committee meetings while leading Marketing team as Co-Chair and PR & Press-Media team as Chair. Since
2020 I have been leading JCLC for JAINA as a chairperson. JCLC Chair and JAINA Director from JSMC gave me
rich experience to work for JAINA initiatives. Since 14 years, I have been a leader serving as a President, Vice
Chairman, Board of Trustees, and Secretary for Chicago Sangh(JSMC).
As JAINA Secretary, my aspiration is to streamline JAINA Backoffice tools, processes, and policies to make
JAINA a modern organization to support Jainism at global level. With my leadership and technical skills, I
would like to introduce process automation so JAINA can scale better to do more at its fullest potential. My
idea about role-based organization (as supposed to current - people-based organization) eliminates peoples
dependencies and provides equal opportunity for all leaders. I am passionate, hardworking, and visionary
leader. I would like to promote shared understanding, trust, and mutual respect amongst all of us within
JAINA, especially within JAINA EC and Board.
Having a visionary and pragmatic team, a long-term goal and sustainable roadmap for JAINA is essential. With
my leadership experience at a large local Jain Sangh as well as at JAINA I could certainly make a difference for
JAINA, Jainism, and Jain values at global level.
